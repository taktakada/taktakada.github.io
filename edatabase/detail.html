Chapter 1 The details of database（COMPADRE）

To use the database

To use COMPADRE and COMADRE databases, you have to register your name, affiliation, e-mail address and to get the login name.

The website for registration is here.

History of COMPADRE

Version 1: Silvertown & Franco (1989) 105 species
Version 2: Salguero-Gomez & Hodgson(2008) 500 species
Version 3: Max Plank Institute (2011–)
Version 4: 2016
Version 5: 2019-

Summary of COMPADRE

・392 studies (781 plant species)
・renewed every 6 months
・8653 matrices
・Taxnonomic names (additional information)
・Phylogenetic tree (additional information)
・Geolocation (additional information)
・Architectural organization (additional information)
・Matrix information (additional information)

Summary (See Salguero-Gomez et al.(2015) J. Ecol.103: 202-218 for the detail)
