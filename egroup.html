Self-introduction

Many mysterious phenomena caused by information transmission among organisms have been known, such as peacock with bright tail, bold unpalatable butterflies and similar palatable butterflies. The information transmission among individuals are called "signaling". I am interested in how signals occur, how signals evolve in a population and how signals affect the population. Now, I am researching mathematical models about community dynamics and evolution of Batesian mimicry.
